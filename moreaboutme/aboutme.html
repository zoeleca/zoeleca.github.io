I'm Zoe but my friends call me zozo!
I was born and grew up in a small but unique archipelago called New Caledonia.

New Caledonia (or "Le Caillou" in french) is a beautiful place.
We have mountains, Beaches, Rivers all with different landscapes
In New Caledonia, we love doing outdoors activities.
We can ride horses, kayak, surf, ride a bike and many more!
I love surfing, hiking and riding bikes !!

An other amazing thing is that we have lots of Sea Turtles and Whales that comes near by or shore ! I was even lucky enough to see one !

Since New Caledonia is a group of island, it is pretty much isolated from the world, but that makes it even more precious.
Nature and a clean Environnement is for us very important



I arrive to "Europe France" in 2018. It was my first time being so far away from my Family.
I moved to a city called Nantes to study "Graphic Design". The study itself was really interesting but the way it was taught was quite disapointing. This same year, I got to learn about French protestation which were quite violent.


Knowing that I couldn't learn more that I already did I decided to change my Major to Korean.
It was a language that I liked and found easy to learn due to the logic of the grammar.
During those three years at University, I learn about transport Strike and lockdown due to the pandemic.
Although it was not easy, I learn a lot about me and I slowly became more independent ^^.
